About DevTools
Three tools, one interface
Resume Generator

Uses Claude AI to craft professional, ATS-friendly resume content. Supports multiple tones, targeted job descriptions, and exports to PDF.

Job Scraper

AI-powered job finder that only shows listings genuinely open to Uganda and East Africa. Region-locked jobs are automatically filtered out. Save any listing directly to the tracker.

Job Tracker

Connected to your local FastAPI backend (http://127.0.0.1:8000). Persists all applications to SQLite and lets you track status through the hiring pipeline.
